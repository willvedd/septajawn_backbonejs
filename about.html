About

Septajawn is an online Septa subway scheduling application. The goal of the project is to deliver a high-quality product that is as accessible and inclusive as possible. In this context, that means functioning adequately on many devices, no monetary barriers, and accessible to those with disabilities. Septajawn was designed and developed by Will Vedder as the Capstone project for his bachelor degree at Temple University.

The project is open source, and can be viewed on the Septajawn github. You're encouraged to download, fork, push the project. If you think this app sucks, feel free to transmit your rage via email at yo@septajawn.com.

Septajawn is built upon the Backbone.js framework and utilizes the default dependencies (Underscore, jQuery). All the data is pulled from a custom made JSON file. Project hosting utilizes github's free gh-pages service.
