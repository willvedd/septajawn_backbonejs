About

Septajawn is an online Septa subway scheduling tool. The goal of the project is to deliver a high-quality product that is as accessible and inclusive as possible. In this context, that means functioning adequately on many devices, no monetary barriers, and accessible to those with disabilities. Septajawn was designed and developed by Temple University student Will Vedder as the Capstone project for his bachelor degree.

This project is open source, and can be viewed on the Septajawn github. You're welcome to download, fork, push the project.

Septajawn is built upon the Backbone javascript framework and utilizes the default dependencies (Underscore, jQuery). All data is pulled from a JSON file. Project hosting utilizes github's free gh-pages service.

If this app makes you feel some type of way, you should share it. Transmit your joy,rage via email at yo@septajawn.com.
